Your Pathway to Research at Dong-A University

The GKS R&D pathway offers a fully funded scholarship combined with early research placement in leading laboratories within the Department of Computer Engineering, enabling students to engage in cutting-edge innovation from the very beginning of their graduate journey.

  • 🌏
    International GKS Scholars Welcome
    Designed for outstanding international students applying through the Global Korea Scholarship (GKS) program, providing access to fully funded graduate research opportunities.
  • 🎓
    7 Research Laboratories
    Students can align with specialized laboratories and faculty research areas before enrollment, enabling early integration into active research projects.
  • 🔬
    Diverse Research Domains
    Our laboratories explore advanced fields including Artificial Intelligence, Digital Twins, Robotics, Computer Vision, Data Science, and Edge Physical AI.
  • 🤝
    Industry & Government Research Projects
    Students participate in collaborative research projects supported by industry partners and national research programs, addressing real-world technological challenges.
  • 🗣️
    Language Requirement
    English proficiency (IELTS 5.5+, TOEFL iBT 71+, or equivalent) is recommended. Students must obtain TOPIK Level 3 to complete the major program.
  • 💬
    Interview Stage
    Shortlisted applicants will participate in an online interview with faculty members to discuss research interests and laboratory alignment.

Eligibility

Core admission requirements

Applicants are advised to verify that they satisfy the following baseline conditions before preparing the final application package.
  • ✓
    Citizenship
    Applicants must be citizens of GKS-eligible countries and must hold non-Korean nationality.
  • ✓
    Academic qualification
    Bachelor’s degree holders may apply for the Master’s track, while Master’s degree holders may apply for the Doctoral track.
  • ✓
    Age limit
    Applicants should be under 40 years of age at the time of application.
  • ✓
    Health condition
    Good physical and mental health is required for scholarship eligibility.
  • ✓
    Prior scholarship history
    Applicants should not have previously received a GKS degree-level scholarship.
  • ✓
    Research readiness
    A strong academic background and clear research potential in computing-related fields are expected.
Recommended emphasis
English test scores and research outputs
  • Applicants with strong English proficiency and demonstrated research outcomes may receive priority consideration during screening.
  • Applicants must achieve TOPIK Level 3 to be officially admitted to the major program.

Workflow

Application process

The original brochure was restructured into a cleaner step-by-step journey for faster review by international applicants.
1

Application Submission

Submit the full application package by postal mail or in person to Dong-A University. The package should include one apostilled original set and one photocopy set of the required documents.

2

Screening & Document Evaluation

The selection committee evaluates academic background, research proposal, and program suitability. Strong English test scores and research outputs may improve competitiveness for the R&D track.

3

First-Round Online Interview

Shortlisted candidates join a Zoom interview with faculty members to assess research potential and communication skills.

4

Result Notification

Screening outcomes are announced individually by email through the Office of International Affairs.

5

Final Selection & Announcement

NIIED conducts the final review and issues the official scholarship award notification. Successful candidates must obtain TOPIK Level 3 within the required period.
